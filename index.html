Instruction for playing Rock, Paper, Scissors

Rock-paper-scissors is a quick win-loose game that that is often used to determine who will go first or who will win some other small priviege. Best for: Two players How to play: In rock-paper-scissors, two players will each randomly choose one of three hand signs: rock (made by making a fist), paper (made by laying your hand flat), or scissors (made by holding out two fingers to look like scissors). Both players show their signs at the same time to see who will win.Here are the rules that determine which sign beats another:

  • Rock wins over scissors (because rock smashes scissors)
  • Scissors wins over paper (because scissors cut paper)
  • Paper wins over rock (because paper covers rock)
